The Challenge

Welcome to the Hangman Game – an interactive and engaging word guessing adventure! Uncover hidden words before the hangman is fully drawn. It's a classic game of deduction, vocabulary, and a little bit of luck, redesigned for a modern experience.

Each round presents you with a hidden word from one of seven exciting categories. Guess letters one by one, but be careful – each wrong guess brings the hangman closer to completion. Can you solve the word before it's too late?
