📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective

Guess the hidden word before the hangman is fully drawn. You have 6 wrong guesses maximum!

⌨️ How to Play

  • Click on the letters or use your keyboard to guess
  • Each correct letter fills in the blanks
  • Each wrong guess adds a part to the hangman
  • Complete the word before 6 wrong guesses to win!

🎁 Free Letters

  • Start with 3 free letters
  • Win games to earn more free letters
  • Use free letters to reveal a random correct letter
  • Build streaks for bonus rewards!

🔥 Streak System

  • Win consecutively to build your streak
  • 3-streak: +2 free letters
  • 5-streak: +3 free letters
  • 10-streak: +4 free letters
  • Perfect game (0 wrong): Extra bonus!
💡 Pro Tip: Use your free letters wisely on longer words!
