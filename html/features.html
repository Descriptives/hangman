Game Features

Everything you need for the ultimate word challenge.

Complete Feature List

🎨

Hangman Drawing

Watch the gallows fill with each incorrect guess. The visual progress adds tension and excitement to every round, with a classic stick figure drawing that appears limb by limb.

⌨️

Dual Keyboard Input

Play your way. Use your physical keyboard for speed or click the on-screen keyboard for a tactile, mouse-friendly experience. Both are fully supported and synchronized.

⏱️

Real-time Timer

Challenge yourself to think fast. A live timer tracks how quickly you solve each word, allowing you to compete against your own personal best times.

🔥

Streak System

Correctly guess words in a row to build a streak. Higher streaks unlock bonus points and provide a satisfying sense of momentum. Don't let a wrong guess break your flow!

🎁

Free Letter Power-ups

Stuck on a word? Earn or unlock power-ups that can reveal a random correct letter in the puzzle, giving you a helpful nudge in the right direction.

📊

Detailed Statistics

Track your journey. The game logs your total games played, wins, losses, current streak, longest streak, and total letters guessed. See your improvement over time.

⚙️

Custom Settings

Personalize your experience. Toggle background music and sound effects on or off, and choose from a selection of visual themes to match your style.

🏆

Achievement System

Go beyond just winning. Unlock special achievements for hitting milestones, like winning your first game, reaching a 10-win streak, or using your first power-up.
